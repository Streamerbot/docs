Enable additional Streamer.bot features by connecting your bot to the Streamer.bot website!

## Configuration

::steps{level=3}

### Connect to your Streamer.bot Website Account

:::navigate
Open the Streamer.bot application
:::

1. Click `Login` in the bottom-left corner to login to your Streamer.bot Website account
1. Complete the OAuth login flow in your web browser

:::success
You can now utilize any features which require a Streamer.bot website connection!
:::
::
