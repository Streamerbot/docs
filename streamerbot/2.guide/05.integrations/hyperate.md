Receive heart rate events from [HypeRate.io](https://hyperate.io){target=\_blank}

## Configuration

::steps{level=3}

### Add HypeRate connection details

:::navigate
In Streamer.bot, navigate to **Integrations > HypeRate.io**
:::

1.  Enter your HypeRate.io ID
    :::tip
    You can enter `internal-testing` in this field to have fake testing data sent to Streamer.bot
    :::

1.  Enable `Auto Connect` to automatically connect to HypeRate.io when Streamer.bot starts up
1.  Enable `Auto Reconnect` to automatically reconnect to HypeRate.io when the connection is disrupted

1.  Done!

    :::success
    You will now receive events from HypeRate!
    :::

::
