The [StreamElements](https://streamelements.com/){target=\_blank} integration allows you to connect Streamer.bot with your StreamElements account to receive tip and merch events.

## Configuration

::steps{level=3}

### Connect to StreamElements

:::navigate
In Streamer.bot, navigate to **Integrations > StreamElements**
:::

1. Click `Connect to StreamElements` to launch the OAuth consent screen
1. Login with StreamElements to connect your account to Streamer.bot
1. Enable `Auto Connect` to automatically connect to StreamElements when Streamer.bot starts up
1. Enable `Auto Reconnect` to automatically reconnect to StreamElements if the connection is disrupted

:::success
StreamElements is now connected to Streamer.bot!
:::

::
