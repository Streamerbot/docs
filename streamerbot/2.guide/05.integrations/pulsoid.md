Receive heart rate events from [Pulsoid](https://pulsoid.net){target=\_blank}

## Configuration

::steps{level=3}

### Connect to Pulsoid

:::navigate
In Streamer.bot, navigate to **Integrations > Pulsoid**
:::

1. Click `Connect to Pulsoid` to launch the OAuth consent screen
1. Login with Pulsoid to connect your account to Streamer.bot
1. Enable `Auto Connect` to automatically connect to Pulsoid when Streamer.bot starts up
1. Enable `Auto Reconnect` to automatically reconnect to Pulsoid if the connection is disrupted

:::success
Pulsoid will now forward events to Streamer.bot!
:::

::
